About Us

Are you ready to be the next superhero in your town? WasteWise is a website where you compete with your friends and neighbors to save the environment! There is a point system that keeps track of how many conscious wise choices you make to be environmentally friendly. Categories include recycling plastics, cans, or glass, composting your food waste, creating your own ecological projects and more. Think you have what it takes? We challenge you to do your best for your home town!
